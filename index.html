During winter rules preferred lies, a player is entitled to mark, lift, clean and replace their ball within 6" and no nearer the hole. Most players put a tee behind their ball and then lift and clean it. The new Arc6 golfing accessory Lift, clean & place means no more guess work and it enhances the etiquette of golf!
